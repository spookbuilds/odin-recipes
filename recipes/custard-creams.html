Custard Creams

Better than the shops can sell!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Custard powder
  • Butter
  • Butter Icing
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Weigh and mix all ingredients
  2. Roll out the dough to around the thickness of a £1 coin
  3. Use a cutter to cut out each biscuit
  4. try to get an even amount!
  5. Bake for roughly 12-15 mins - till golden
  6. Cool on wire rack
  7. Once cool, spread generous amount of butter icing on one biscuit
  8. Adding another biscuit on top to sandwich it together
  9. Enjoy!
