Pasta Salad

Delicious Pasta Salad that is easy to make and can have plenty leftover for the following day!

Ingredients

  • 200g Pasta
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Bell Peppers
  • Spring Onion
  • Cucumber
  • Mayonnaise
  • Optional Salad Cream

Steps

  1. Weigh Pasta and set to boil for 12 mins.
  2. Chop desired lettuce
  3. Chop desired tomatoes
  4. Chop desired bell peppers
  5. Chop desired spring onion
  6. Chop desired cucumber
  7. Mix all salad in a bowl with 100g of mayonnaise and optional 50g of salad cream
  8. Once pasta has cooked, drain and run under cold water till pasta is cool.
  9. Mix pasta in with salad
  10. Serve, or store in fridge for later.
